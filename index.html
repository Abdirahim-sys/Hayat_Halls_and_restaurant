about us

elcome to Hayat Halls and Restaurant, a culinary gem in the heart of Muqdisho. Our mission is simple: to offer a delightful escape into the world of flavors. With skilled chefs and a warm ambiance, we've curated a space where every visit becomes a memorable celebration.

At Hayat, we believe in the power of good food to bring people together. Our diverse menu reflects a commitment to quality and creativity, ensuring that each dish is a journey for your taste buds. Whether you're a local or a visitor, join us for a unique dining experience that transcends the ordinary.
